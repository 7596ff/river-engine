# Gaza

**First noted:** 2026-02-20
**Status:** ongoing

## Summary
Israel's assault on Gaza continues despite ceasefire (in effect since ~Oct 2025). Lancet study: 75,000+ violent deaths in first 16 months (vs 49,000 reported by Palestinian Health Ministry). More than half were women, seniors, children. Since ceasefire: 642+ killed (197 children), average 5/day. Only 43% of agreed aid trucks allowed in, 15% of fuel trucks. 20,000 patients need urgent evacuation; 3,300+ died waiting. 10,000+ bodies remain under rubble. Israel destroyed Rafah crossing (now military checkpoint). At least 2 of 8 refugee camps erased.

Trump administration proposed turning Gaza into "upscale seaside resort with gleaming skyscrapers" at inaugural Board of Peace meeting. $10B pledged (not Congress-approved). Pope refused to join. Israel joined.

Israel also systematically violating ceasefires in both Lebanon and Gaza. In Lebanon: >10,000 attacks since Hezbollah ceasefire (Nov 2024). In Gaza: 642+ killed since ceasefire.

## Timeline
- **2026-02-02:** Israeli forces will reportedly reopen Rafah crossing into Egypt for only 6 hours/day with strict travel restrictions. Crossing has been shuttered for 2 years due to Israel's war and blockade; being reopened as part of ceasefire brokered by Trump admin. Within new restrictions: 150 people allowed to leave Gaza through southern Rafah crossing, 50 individuals permitted to enter territory per day (Al Jazeera). Tens of thousands of sick and severely wounded Palestinians, including children, eagerly waiting to leave Gaza for urgent medical care. Gaza health officials estimate over 1,200 Palestinians have died in Gaza while waiting for medical transfer due to Israel's closure of Rafah crossing. Al Jazeera reports ~12,000 medical staff in Egypt preparing to receive medical evacuees from Gaza: hundreds of hospitals and ambulances ready to treat Palestinian patients, including children who've lost limbs in Israeli strikes. Mohammed Mahdi (Khan Younis, getting ready to evacuate with patient): "It feels like a dream, especially as my father — we had lost hope of him receiving treatment, and then they suddenly called us and told him to come to receive treatment and travel. This system, this system is a dream. A dream, I swear." Israeli forces continue strikes across Gaza, killing at least 30 people including 6 children this weekend, in violation of ceasefire. Some victims were taking shelter in tents. Reportedly one of deadliest Israeli airstrikes on Gaza in months — even during supposed "ceasefire," Israel continues systematic killing.
- **2026-02-03:** Israeli airstrikes killed at least 4 Palestinians. Palestinians gathered at Nasser Hospital in Khan Younis to mourn 30-month-old boy Eyad al-Rabaya killed by Israeli fire. Omar al-Rabaya (relative): "Gunboats fired out toward the regional park. An explosion took place in a tent. I came looking and found a child without a head inside the tent." Israeli forces detonated and destroyed Morag Water Station in Rafah — supplied water to roughly one-third of Rafah's residents before war. Israel announced ban on Doctors Without Borders (MSF) from operating in Gaza after charity refused to provide Israeli authorities list of its Palestinian staff. MSF currently provides at least 20% of hospital beds in Gaza. USAID staffers in early 2024 drafted warning to senior Biden admin officials that Israeli attacks had turned northern Gaza into an "Apocalyptic Wasteland." Internal memo laid out harrowing accounts by UN observers: human femur and other bones in streets, dead bodies abandoned in cars, "catastrophic human needs, particularly for food and safe drinking water." Reuters reports U.S. Ambassador to Israel Jack Lew and his deputy blocked memo from disseminating within U.S. government because they believed it wasn't balanced. Guardian reports U.S. disaster response firm Gothams LLC seeking deal with White House for seven-year monopoly over trucking/logistics plan for Trump's Board of Peace in Gaza. Plan would guarantee Gothams LLC 300% profits and charge for use of warehousing/distribution system. Gothams LLC has also supported operations at notorious South Florida ICE jail, dubbed "Alligator Alcatraz."
- **2026-02-05:** 25 Palestinians returned through Rafah border crossing early Feb 5 — first returnees since Israel partially reopened crossing Monday after nearly 2 years. Returnees described harrowing journey with severe Israeli restrictions: hours of interrogation, physical abuse, confiscation of personal belongings. One woman stripped of essential medications, toys and cellphones for children/siblings. 23 Palestinians killed in one day by Israeli tank fire and airstrikes. Among dead: medic Hussein al-Semiry, killed by second strike while transporting wounded from Khan Younis. Raed al-Nems (medic): "We were surprised today by the targeting of our colleague medic, Hussein al-Semiry, who was martyred while carrying out his humanitarian duty of transporting the wounded from Khan Younis. These attacks must stop. International law provides protection to providers of health and ambulance services."
- **2026-02-06:** Global Sumud Flotilla (South Africa) announced Thursday plans for largest-ever direct action to break Israel's Gaza siege with humanitarian aid. Thousands of people from over 100 countries, over 100 boats, more than 1,000 doctors/nurses/health professionals in coordinated nonviolent response to "genocide, siege, mass starvation and the destruction of civilian life in Gaza." Sets sail March 2026. Sumeyra Akdeniz Ordu (organizer): "We will sail with, this time, thousands of participants, including more than a thousand doctors, nurses, health professionals, over a hundred boats." Nkosi Zwelivelile "Mandla" Mandela (South African MP, Nelson Mandela's grandson, flotilla organizer) planning to join — was abducted at sea by Israeli forces during October 2025 attempt to sail into Gaza with humanitarian aid. Mandela: "As a collective across the globe, we can isolate apartheid Israel, collapse it and bring it to its knees, just as we did to apartheid South Africa. We therefore want to take this opportunity, comrades and friends, to call on the entire global community. The registration is now open for Mission 2026." Strategic framing: explicitly connecting to anti-apartheid movement; using South Africa's successful isolation campaign as model. Mechanism: mass civil disobedience + humanitarian mission + international coalition building to challenge siege through direct action and global attention.
